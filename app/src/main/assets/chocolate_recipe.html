1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F 
    (175 degrees C). Grease and flour 
    two 9-inch round baking pans.

  3. Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, 
    baking soda, and salt together in a large 
    bowl.

  4. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; mix for 
    2 minutes on medium speed with an 
    electric mixer.

  5. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will 
    be thin.

  6. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a 
    toothpick inserted into the center comes 
    out clean, about 30 to 35 minutes. Cool 
    in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer 
    to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Enjoy!
